## 🎯 Core Research Question

> *To what extent are Grade 10 teachers trained for pathway-specific CBC instruction across STEM, Social Sciences, and Arts & Sports Science — and what factors explain variation in preparedness?*

---

## 📊 Key Finding from Literature (Before Primary Data Collection)

- Only **3%** of Kenyan educators feel adequately prepared for CBC (Mwang'ombe, 2021)
- **36%** of teachers had not engaged in any retooling training as of 2024 (KNEC)
- **77%** of junior secondary schools identify stakeholder attitudes as a barrier (IJRISS, 2025)
- **68%** of teachers expected further training but had not received it (2024)
- Grade 10 (Senior School) started January 2026 — **zero empirical studies exist at this level**
