# Research Questions
## With Justification from Literature

---

## RQ1: Training Adequacy by Pathway
> *To what extent are Grade 10 teachers trained for pathway-specific CBC instruction across STEM, Social Sciences, and Arts & Sports Science?*

**Justification:** No existing study disaggregates teacher preparedness by CBC pathway. Mogere & Mbatanu (2025) project teacher numbers per pathway but do not assess training quality. All other studies treat "CBC teachers" as one group.

**Variables:** Training frequency × Pathway type × Self-rated preparedness

---

## RQ2: Gaps in In-Service Training
> *What are the perceived gaps in in-service training content, methodology, and frequency among Grade 10 teachers?*

**Justification:** KNUT (2022) and Kilifi County study (2025) both show training design matters significantly (p=0.000). Yet no study has examined what Grade 10 teachers specifically feel is missing from their training, especially for new pathway subjects never taught under 8-4-4.

**Variables:** Training modality × Content relevance × Frequency × Self-efficacy outcomes

---

## RQ3: Self-Efficacy Variation
> *How does teacher self-efficacy vary across different CBC senior school pathways and regions?*

**Justification:** Self-efficacy is cited theoretically in 8 studies but never directly measured using validated instruments at secondary/senior school level in Kenya. Bandura (1997) predicts that self-efficacy — shaped by mastery experiences and social modeling — will vary by subject domain and contextual support.

**Variables:** TSES scores × Pathway × County/Region × Training received

---

## RQ4: School-Level Moderating Factors
> *What school-level factors (head teacher support, resources, mentorship) moderate effective CBC delivery at Grade 10?*

**Justification:** Head teacher capacity is mentioned in 6 studies but never studied at SSS level where three simultaneous pathways create unprecedented complexity. Fullan's change model predicts institutional factors are as important as individual teacher factors.

**Variables:** Head teacher CBC literacy × Resource availability × Mentorship frequency × Delivery quality
